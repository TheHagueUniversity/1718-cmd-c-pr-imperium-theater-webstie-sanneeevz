The State of Mary

What did you think, the first time you saw me? Did you spy the devil’s daughter they’d warned you about?
Two iconic queens in a bloody power struggle have to deal with their faiths, the interests of their people, and of the men that surround them… Mary’s asylum puts Elizabeth in a state of defence. Family loyalty and duty to the kingdom, fear of death and child envy define the outcome of this intimate confrontation.

Based on: Schiller’s Maria Stuart
Directed by: Arne Sybren Postma
Developed by: Herman Duchenne René Huinen, Els van Midden, Michel Lausberg, Alex Jan Friso en Carlijn Pereira
